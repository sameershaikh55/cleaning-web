WHY CHOOSE US?

SKIP THE MIDDLEMAN.

Did you know that most cleaning and maid services in Calgary outsource their cleaning to third parties? That’s right! If you have ever wondered why the quality of the cleaning you recieve is never consistent, its because there is a good chance there is a different company actually doing the cleaning. Not only do we hire and vet our own cleaners here at GoMaid, we also train them according to our strict cleaning standards. This means that with the exception of acute circumstances, you will likely have the same cleaners every time! No more guess work, you deserve consistency.





THOROUGH CHECKLIST. CUSTOMIZED SERVICE

All our cleaners are trained using a thorough checklist that they’re required to bring with them to every service. This checklist is then provided to our client after the service is complete. We are dedicated to providing our clients with the best possible experience.
